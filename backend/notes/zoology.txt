Zoology Notes

1. Introduction:
   - Zoology is the study of animals, their structure, physiology, development, and classification.

2. Animal Classification:
   - Kingdom Animalia is divided based on body structure, symmetry, body cavity, and segmentation.
   - Major Phyla: Porifera, Cnidaria, Platyhelminthes, Nematoda, Annelida, Arthropoda, Mollusca, Echinodermata, Chordata.

3. Body Systems:
   - Digestive System: Converts food into energy and nutrients.
   - Circulatory System: Transports oxygen and nutrients.
   - Nervous System: Controls body responses and actions.
   - Reproductive System: Enables procreation.

4. Animal Physiology:
   - Homeostasis: Maintaining internal balance.
   - Metabolism: Sum of all biochemical processes.

5. Evolution:
   - Charles Darwin proposed Natural Selection as a mechanism for evolution.
   - Adaptations lead to species survival.

6. Importance:
   - Helps understand biodiversity, ecology, and human evolution.
