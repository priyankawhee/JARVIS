Cryptocurrencies Notes

1. Introduction:
   - Cryptocurrencies are decentralized digital currencies secured by cryptography.
   - Bitcoin was the first cryptocurrency, introduced in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto.

2. Key Concepts:
   - Blockchain: A distributed ledger technology that records transactions.
   - Mining: The process of validating transactions and adding them to the blockchain.
   - Wallets: Digital tools to store crypto assets.
   - Private and Public Keys: Used for authentication and security.

3. Popular Cryptocurrencies:
   - Bitcoin (BTC)
   - Ethereum (ETH)
   - Ripple (XRP)
   - Litecoin (LTC)
   - Cardano (ADA)

4. Consensus Mechanisms:
   - Proof of Work (PoW)
   - Proof of Stake (PoS)
   - Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS)

5. Applications:
   - Smart Contracts, NFTs, DeFi (Decentralized Finance), Cross-border Payments.
